BWxD 2017 Conference

Think with us:

On the occasion of Better World’s 10th anniversary in 2017, we decided to organize a different kind of conference in aims of breaking the conversational mold. The 2017 theme, Blocks, Bytes, and Bars, used symbols to provide an alternative method of conceiving the design process. These three objects represent design’s traditional past, technological present, and aspirational future. See the past speakers that we had the honor of hosting below!
